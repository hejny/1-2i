## Installation Instructions

Installing our digital devices wallpaper is easy! Here's what you'll need:

- Wallpaper adhesive
- Wallpaper smoothing tool
- Measuring tape
- Scissors
- Utility knife

Follow these steps:

1. Measure the height and width of your wall to determine how much wallpaper you'll need.
2. Cut the wallpaper into strips that are slightly longer than your wall height.
3. Apply wallpaper adhesive to the back of the first strip using a roller or brush.
4. Carefully position the strip on the wall, leaving a small overlap at the top and bottom.
5. Use the smoothing tool to remove any air bubbles and ensure a smooth surface.
6. Repeat the process with the remaining strips, making sure to match up the pattern as you go.
7. Use the utility knife to trim any excess wallpaper from the edges.
