## Installation Tips

1. Prepare your walls by cleaning them thoroughly and ensuring they are smooth and dry.
2. Measure your walls carefully to determine how much wallpaper you will need.
3. Apply wallpaper paste directly to the wall using a roller or brush.
4. Hang your first strip of wallpaper, making sure it is level and aligned with the edge of the wall.
5. Continue hanging strips of wallpaper, matching up the pattern as you go.
6. Use a wallpaper smoother to remove any air bubbles and ensure a smooth finish.
