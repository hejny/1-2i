# Malware Madness: A Dark and Edgy Background for Your Website

Welcome to Malware Madness, the perfect background for those who want to add a touch of edginess to their website. Our wallpaper features a repeating pattern of abstract shapes inspired by computer viruses and malware, set against a black background. 

## Why Choose Malware Madness?

Our wallpaper is not just any ordinary background; it's a statement piece that adds personality to your website. Here are some reasons why you should choose Malware Madness:

- Unique Design: Our wallpaper is inspired by computer viruses and malware, making it stand out from other backgrounds.
- Edgy Look: The black background and abstract shapes give your website a dark and edgy look that's perfect for tech-related industries.
- Versatile: Whether you're creating a website for a cybersecurity firm or a gaming company, our wallpaper fits any industry.

## User Stories
