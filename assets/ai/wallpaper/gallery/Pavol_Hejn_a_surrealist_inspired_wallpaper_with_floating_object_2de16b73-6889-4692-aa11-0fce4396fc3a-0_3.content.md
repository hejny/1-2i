## Installation

Installing our Dreamscape wallpaper is easy and straightforward. Simply follow these steps:

1. Clean the wall surface and make sure it is dry.
2. Measure the wall and cut the wallpaper to the appropriate size, leaving a few extra inches on all sides.
3. Apply wallpaper paste to the back of the wallpaper.
4. Carefully position the wallpaper on the wall, starting from the top and smoothing out any bubbles or wrinkles.
5. Trim the excess wallpaper using a sharp knife or scissors.
