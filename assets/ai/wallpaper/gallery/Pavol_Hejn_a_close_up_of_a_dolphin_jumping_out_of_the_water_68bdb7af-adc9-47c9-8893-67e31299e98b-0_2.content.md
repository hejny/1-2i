## About Dolphins

Dolphins are one of the most intelligent and social marine mammals, known for their playful nature and acrobatic skills. They are found in oceans and seas around the world, and there are over 40 different species of dolphins.

Did you know that dolphins use echolocation to navigate and communicate with each other? They emit high-pitched sounds that bounce off objects and return as echoes, allowing them to "see" their surroundings and locate prey.
