# Space Station Background for Your Next Video Call

Are you tired of the same old boring backgrounds on your video calls? Look no further! Our space station background is the perfect addition to spice up your virtual meetings.

## Features

- Pixel art depiction of a space station
- Blocky shapes and limited colors create a retro and futuristic feel
- High-quality image resolution for a professional look
- Compatible with most video conferencing platforms

## User Stories

*"I used this background for a work meeting and received so many compliments! It definitely made the call more interesting."* - Jane, Marketing Manager

*"My friends and I had a virtual game night and this background really set the mood. We felt like we were in our own little spaceship!"* - Mike, Gamer

## How to Use

1. Download the image by right-clicking and selecting "Save Image As"
2. Open your video conferencing platform of choice (Zoom, Teams, etc.)
3. Navigate to the virtual background settings
4. Upload the downloaded image as your new background
