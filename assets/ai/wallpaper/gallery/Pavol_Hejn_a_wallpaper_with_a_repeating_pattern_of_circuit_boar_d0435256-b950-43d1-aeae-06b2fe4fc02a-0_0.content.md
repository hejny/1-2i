## Installation

Our "Circuit Board" wallpaper is easy to install with paste-the-wall application. Simply follow these steps:

1. Measure your wall to determine how many rolls you will need.
2. Prepare your wall by cleaning it thoroughly and filling in any holes or cracks.
3. Apply wallpaper paste directly to the wall using a roller.
4. Hang each strip of wallpaper, matching up the pattern as you go.
5. Smooth out any bubbles or wrinkles with a wallpaper smoother or brush.
