## What are Black Holes?

Black holes are incredibly dense regions in space where the gravitational pull is so strong that nothing, not even light, can escape. They form when massive stars collapse in on themselves, creating a singularity at their center. The event horizon is the point of no return, beyond which anything that enters is pulled inexorably towards the singularity.
