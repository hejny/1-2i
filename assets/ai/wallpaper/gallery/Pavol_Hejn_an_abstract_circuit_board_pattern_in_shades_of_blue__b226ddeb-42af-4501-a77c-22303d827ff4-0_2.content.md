## Installation

Installing our abstract circuit board wallpaper is easy and straightforward. Simply follow these steps:

1. Measure the wall(s) where you want to install the wallpaper.
2. Cut the wallpaper to the appropriate size.
3. Apply wallpaper paste to the back of the wallpaper.
4. Carefully position the wallpaper on the wall, smoothing out any bubbles or wrinkles.
5. Trim any excess wallpaper from the edges.

For more detailed instructions, please refer to our [installation guide](#installation-guide).
