## What is a Space Elevator?

A space elevator is a theoretical transportation system that could revolutionize space travel. It consists of a cable stretching from Earth into orbit, with a counterweight at the end to keep it taut. 

## The Benefits of a Space Elevator

With a space elevator, we could drastically reduce the cost of launching objects into space. Instead of using expensive rockets, we could simply use the elevator to transport people and materials into orbit. This would make space travel more accessible to everyone.
