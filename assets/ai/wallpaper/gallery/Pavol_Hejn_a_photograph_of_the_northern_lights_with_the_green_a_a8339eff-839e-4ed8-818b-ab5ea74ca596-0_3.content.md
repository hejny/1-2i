## About the Aurora Borealis

The Aurora Borealis, also known as the Northern Lights, is a natural light display that occurs in the polar regions. It is caused by charged particles from the sun colliding with atoms in the Earth's atmosphere. The resulting energy release creates a beautiful display of colors, including green, pink, purple, and blue.
