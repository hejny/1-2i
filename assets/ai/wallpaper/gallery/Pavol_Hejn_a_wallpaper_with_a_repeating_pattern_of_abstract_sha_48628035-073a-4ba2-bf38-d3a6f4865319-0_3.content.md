# Malware Protection

Welcome to Malware Madness, your one-stop-shop for all things cybersecurity! Our mission is to keep you safe from the latest threats lurking in the digital world. 

## Our Wallpaper

Our website features a striking wallpaper with a repeating pattern of abstract shapes inspired by computer viruses and malware, set against a black background. This design serves as a constant reminder of the dangers that exist online and the importance of staying vigilant.

## Resources

## User Stories

## Contact Us
