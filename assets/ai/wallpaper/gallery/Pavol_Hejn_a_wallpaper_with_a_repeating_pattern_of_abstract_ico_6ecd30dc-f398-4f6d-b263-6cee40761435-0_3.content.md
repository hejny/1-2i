# Tech Icons Wallpaper

Welcome to our collection of tech-inspired wallpapers! Our "Tech Icons" wallpaper features a repeating pattern of abstract icons and symbols commonly used in the tech industry, such as computer chips and power buttons. This wallpaper is perfect for anyone who loves technology and wants to bring a touch of it into their home or office.

## Installation

Our Tech Icons wallpaper is easy to install and can be applied to any smooth surface. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Clean the surface where you will be applying the wallpaper.
2. Measure the area and cut the wallpaper to size.
3. Apply adhesive to the back of the wallpaper or directly to the wall.
4. Smooth out any bubbles or wrinkles using a squeegee or flat tool.
5. Enjoy your new tech-inspired wallpaper!
