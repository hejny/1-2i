## Installation Tips

Installing wallpaper can seem like a daunting task, but with a little bit of patience and preparation, it can be a fun DIY project. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Measure your walls carefully to determine how much wallpaper you will need.
2. Prep your walls by cleaning them thoroughly and filling in any holes or cracks.
3. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for applying the wallpaper adhesive and hanging the wallpaper.
4. Take your time and work carefully to avoid air bubbles and wrinkles.
