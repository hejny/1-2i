# Geometric Wallpaper

Decorate your home or office with our stylish geometric wallpaper. Our wallpaper features a repeating pattern of simple geometric shapes, creating a modern and sophisticated look. The monochromatic color palette, available in grey or blue, makes it easy to match with any decor.

## Features

- Repeating pattern of simple geometric shapes
- Monochromatic color palette in grey or blue
- Easy to match with any decor
- Perfect for homes or offices

## Installation

Installing our wallpaper is easy! Simply follow these steps:

1. Clean the wall surface where you will be applying the wallpaper.
2. Measure the height and width of the wall to determine how much wallpaper you will need.
3. Cut the wallpaper to the appropriate size.
4. Apply wallpaper paste to the back of the wallpaper.
5. Carefully apply the wallpaper to the wall, smoothing out any bubbles or wrinkles.
6. Trim any excess wallpaper from the edges.

## Maintenance

Our wallpaper is easy to maintain. Simply wipe down with a damp cloth to remove any dirt or dust. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that may damage the wallpaper.
