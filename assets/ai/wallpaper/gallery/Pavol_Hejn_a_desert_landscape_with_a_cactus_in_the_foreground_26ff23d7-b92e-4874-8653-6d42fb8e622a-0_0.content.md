## Installation
Installing our desert oasis wallpaper is easy and can be done in just a few steps:
1. Clean the wall surface where the wallpaper will be applied.
2. Measure the wall and cut the wallpaper to the appropriate size.
3. Apply wallpaper paste to the back of the wallpaper.
4. Carefully apply the wallpaper to the wall, smoothing out any wrinkles or bubbles.
5. Trim any excess wallpaper from the edges.

## Care Instructions
To keep your desert oasis wallpaper looking great for years to come, follow these care instructions:

- Do not expose to direct sunlight for extended periods of time
