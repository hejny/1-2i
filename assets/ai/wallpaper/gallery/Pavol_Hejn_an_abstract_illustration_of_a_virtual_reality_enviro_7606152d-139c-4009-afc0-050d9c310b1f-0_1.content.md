## What is Virtual Reality?

Virtual Reality (VR) is a computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional environment that can be interacted with in a seemingly real or physical way. It is an immersive experience that allows users to feel like they are actually present in a different world.

## Benefits of Virtual Reality

- Provides a unique and engaging way to learn and train
- Offers a safe and controlled environment for simulations and experiments
- Can enhance entertainment experiences such as gaming and movies
- Has potential applications in healthcare, therapy, and rehabilitation
