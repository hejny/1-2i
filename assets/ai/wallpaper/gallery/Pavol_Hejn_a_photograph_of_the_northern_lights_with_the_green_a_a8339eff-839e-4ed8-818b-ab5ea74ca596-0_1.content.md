## About the Aurora Borealis

The Aurora Borealis, also known as the Northern Lights, is a natural light display that occurs in the polar regions. It is caused by the interaction between charged particles from the sun and the Earth's magnetic field. The result is a beautiful display of colors that dance across the night sky.
