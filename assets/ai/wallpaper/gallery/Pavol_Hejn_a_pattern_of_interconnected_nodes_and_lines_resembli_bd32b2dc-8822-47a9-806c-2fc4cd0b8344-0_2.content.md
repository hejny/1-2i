## Installation

Once you have downloaded the wallpaper, follow these steps to set it as your background:

3. Click on "Background" from the left-hand menu.
4. Click on the "Browse" button and select the downloaded wallpaper file.
5. Click on "Choose picture" to set the wallpaper as your background.
