# TechHub Community

Welcome to TechHub, a community for technology enthusiasts! Our website is designed to provide you with the latest news and updates in the tech industry. We are dedicated to creating a space where individuals can connect, share ideas, and learn from one another.

## Our Wallpaper

As you browse through our website, you will notice a wallpaper with a repeating pattern of circuit board components, in shades of black, grey, and neon green. This wallpaper represents our love for technology and innovation. It's a subtle nod to the intricate details that make up the technology we use every day.
