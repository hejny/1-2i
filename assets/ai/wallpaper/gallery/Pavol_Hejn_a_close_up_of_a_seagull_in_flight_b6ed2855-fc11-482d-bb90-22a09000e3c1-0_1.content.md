## Installation

Installing our seagull wallpaper is easy and hassle-free. Simply follow these steps:

1. Clean the wall surface and let it dry completely.
2. Measure the wall and cut the wallpaper to the appropriate size, leaving an extra inch at the top and bottom.
3. Apply wallpaper paste to the back of the wallpaper using a roller or brush.
4. Carefully place the wallpaper on the wall, starting from the top and smoothing out any bubbles or wrinkles as you go.
5. Trim the excess wallpaper at the top and bottom using a sharp knife or scissors.
