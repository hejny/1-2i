## Installation Guide

1. Clean and prepare the surface where you will apply the wallpaper.
2. Measure the area and cut the wallpaper accordingly, leaving an extra inch on all sides.
3. Apply wallpaper paste to the back of the wallpaper using a roller or brush.
4. Carefully align the wallpaper with the surface and smooth out any bubbles or wrinkles.
5. Trim the excess wallpaper using a sharp blade or scissors.
6. Allow the wallpaper to dry completely before enjoying your new stylish space!
